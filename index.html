Yuva: youth (Hindi)


Yuvaan, the official literary fest of Delhi Technological University, laid the foundation of a legacy with it’s premier edition in 2017. Deriving from youth, the fest encapsulates the vigour, boldness, spontaneity and needless to say the youthfulness, this transforming into the voice, shaping the future; the perfect amalgamation of technology, science and literature. Come, experience and discover the power of words.

20k+ expected footfall

Yuvaan attracts participants and visitors from institutions all over Delhi and the NCR region. With events for both the literature oriented and non oriented, Yuvaan promises to deliver more.

20+ events

With a multitude of events for everyone, Yuvaan provides an opportunity to get spoiled with choice! Battle with words, laugh your heart out and unwind in the bonfire night, a magical experience.

6+ celebrities/speakers

A stellar celebrity line up has graced the fest in the past: authors, motivational speakers, national bravehearts and the giants of journalism, we’ve had them all. Watch this space for the big reveal!
